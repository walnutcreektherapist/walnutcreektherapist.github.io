About

An integrated clinician

For over 14 years I've worked with couples and individuals, combining structured, skills-based methods with measurement-based and feedback-informed care. I see people in person, in a real office in downtown Walnut Creek — increasingly rare, and it matters. I treat the relationship and the trauma underneath it rather than sending you to three providers who don't talk to each other, and I'm often the one who catches what others have missed.

Common questions

Frequently asked

What does therapy cost?
Individual sessions are $275 per clinical hour; couples are $305. No insurance is accepted — the practice is private-pay and out-of-network. A monthly superbill is available for reimbursement, and you can pay with an HSA or FSA card.
What are your hours?
Weekdays. The first session is at 9am and the last at 5:45pm.
Do you meet in person?
Yes — that's how I work. Sessions are in person, in a real office in downtown Walnut Creek. Couples therapy is in person only; for individuals, secure video is available across California when meeting in person isn't possible.
How do I get started?
Book a free 15-minute consultation through the scheduler, or call (925) 315-8205.
