How I work

One clinician for the whole picture

I treat the whole person, not a symptom — making room for everything that's affecting you, from anxiety, trauma, and sleep to the goals that matter to you, whether that's being a steadier parent, getting unstuck at work, or simply feeling more like yourself again. Here's what that looks like in practice:

Measured

We set real goals and check progress against them, so you'll see whether therapy is working — and if it stalls, I'll tell you.

Skills-based

Couples build concrete skills in sequence; individuals work through a focused plan of what matters most. Either way, the real work happens in session — you leave with more than insight.

Integrated

I treat the whole person, not a single symptom — your history, your health, your sleep, and the goals you actually care about — not a stack of providers who don't talk to each other.

Honest

Straight answers about your progress, including when it's time to change course.
