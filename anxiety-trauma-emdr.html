Treatment that targets the trauma, not just the talk

For PTSD, panic, and anxiety I draw on EMDR, the Flash technique, exposure therapy, Cognitive Processing Therapy, and CBT. EMDR helps your brain reprocess distressing memories so they lose their grip; Flash makes that gentler by lowering the intensity before we work with the memory at all. The point is to change how those experiences live in your body and mind, not only to discuss them.

I track your symptoms with standard measures so we can both see what's improving and adjust if it isn't. And because I work as an integrated clinician, I tend to notice what others have missed — undiagnosed ADHD, depression, even sleep apnea behind years of fatigue — and get you to the right care alongside the work we do together.
